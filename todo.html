About the App

The To-Do List Creator is a simple yet powerful tool to help you manage your tasks efficiently.

Whether you're planning daily chores, work-related tasks, or personal goals, this app allows you to add, delete, and organize tasks easily. It's designed to be intuitive and user-friendly, making task management a breeze.

Features:

  • Simple task addition and deletion
  • Responsive design that works on mobile and desktop
  • Clean and minimalistic interface for easy task management

How It Works:

Just type in your task, hit "Add," and start organizing your day. You can delete tasks as you complete them, ensuring that your to-do list is always up to date.
